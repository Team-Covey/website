Before You Message

What to include

A little context helps the team respond quickly and route requests to the right person.

Subject: partnership, volunteer, media, or technical question.
Timeline: include event dates or deadline expectations if relevant.
Scope: provide links, assets, or the exact support you are requesting.
Best contact: include your preferred reply channel and timezone.
