Who RFDS Is

A lifeline for regional and remote Australia

The Royal Flying Doctor Service delivers essential aeromedical, primary health, and emergency support to communities where distance can make care harder to access.

Emergency response: critical care and aeromedical retrieval support when every minute matters.
Remote healthcare: frontline services for people living and working far from major hospitals.
Community impact: trusted teams supporting regional and remote Australians every year.
