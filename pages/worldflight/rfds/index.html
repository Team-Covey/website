WorldFlight x RFDS

Our Goal for the Royal Flying Doctor Service

Everything about Team Covey's WorldFlight campaign is designed to support awareness and fundraising for RFDS.

Why RFDS is our core focus

RFDS delivers vital health services across regional, rural, and remote Australia. Through WorldFlight, Team Covey uses continuous simulator operations and live broadcasting to channel community support into a cause with clear and practical impact.

How the WorldFlight effort is structured

The mission is tailored to produce both fundraising and sustained visibility for RFDS.

Consistent awareness: RFDS remains a central message throughout every leg and livestream segment.
Donation moments: milestones, route progress, and campaign updates are used to encourage focused giving.
Community education: stream content highlights why remote healthcare access matters across Australia.
Transparent reporting: supporters can follow route progress and campaign outcomes as the event unfolds.
