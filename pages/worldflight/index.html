    How It Works

    Event flow from launch to finish

    The event is run in structured stages so the stream and simulator can stay stable for the full week.

    Pre-flight build week

    Scenario checks, route validation, crew assignments, and broadcast scene rehearsals.

    Global launch leg

    The stream opens with team introductions, charity context, and departure from the starting hub.

    Continuous relay operations

    Pilots, dispatch, and tech rotate while each leg is completed and handed to the next crew.

    Final approach and wrap-up

    The team lands the final leg live, closes the campaign totals, and publishes post-event highlights.
