About Us

Who we are

WorldFlight Team Covey was originally founded with the vision of operating a 737-800 simulator powered by ProSim. However, an unexpected and exciting opportunity arose to acquire the legacy QFA25 747-400 simulator, the very aircraft that ultimately carried us on our debut journey around the world. While that first global adventure came with its share of challenges, it marked the beginning of something truly special for our team. Following a gap year in 2024 due to crew shortages and simulator delays, Caleb dedicated extensive time and effort to restoring, refining, and enhancing the simulator. His commitment ensured that Team Covey returned stronger than ever in 2025, successfully flying every leg of the journey around the world, often ahead of schedule. Our story is one of adaptability, perseverance, and passion for aviation, and we’re only just getting started.
