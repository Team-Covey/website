Our Story

Built on passion,
tested by perseverance

WorldFlight Team Covey started with a vision to fly a 737-800 simulator. An unexpected opportunity changed everything, we acquired the legendary QFA25 747-400, the machine that carried us around the world on our debut. Our story is one of adaptability, grit, and an unwavering love of aviation.
